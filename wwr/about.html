About Us - Rapids Rafting Company

We have the best overall adventure experience in the world, we give you the chance to work as a team, feel the adrenaline of hard work, act fast to find a quick solution to everyday problems, also used to survive in the corporate world. And as a plus, you get to enjoy mother nature and her beatiful sights. All the activities propperly supervised and controlled by our team of rafting experts.

History

We started as an adventure experience available for friends and family back in 1985, with only 2 rafts covering two rivers and a couple of trainers. Now we offer adventures, corporate workshops and even sports adrenaline, we have all the tools to help you and your team to become stronger.
